How We Started

Haltons Pharmacy was founded with a vision to provide accessible and reliable healthcare services to the Kenyan community. Over the years, it has established a strong presence with 19 pharmacies nationwide, earning trust and respect from the communities it serves.

As the healthcare landscape evolved, Haltons recognized the need to adapt to changing customer expectations and the rise of digital solutions. This awareness sparked a transformative journey aimed at enhancing service delivery through innovative technology, reflecting its commitment to meeting the healthcare needs of Kenyans effectively.

We Are Trusted Company

At Haltons Pharmacy, we pride ourselves on being a trusted provider of healthcare services in Kenya. Our commitment to quality and reliability has earned us the confidence of our customers, ensuring they receive the best care and support.

With years of dedicated service, we have built a reputation for excellence, characterized by our knowledgeable staff, a wide range of quality products, and a focus on customer satisfaction. We continuously strive to enhance our services to meet the evolving needs of the communities we serve.
